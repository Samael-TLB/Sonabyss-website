Clash Of Titans

Description

Close event. Individual event( only for the prelims round and if selected for the final round one must bring either a professor or a PhD scholar as a partner).

Rules

  • There will be a preliminary round in the form of an extempore to filter out a total of 6 contestants.

  • Each of the selected 6 contestants then shall have to participate in the final round along with a professor or a PhD scholar as a team.

  • The final round will be a conventional debate competition for which the topic will be provided beforehand and the teams have to decide whether to compete for or against the given topic.

  • The contestants can use either Hindi or English but should refrain from using Hinglish.
