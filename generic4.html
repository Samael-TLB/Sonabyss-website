Short Story

Description

Short story writing is a competition where the participants are required to write short stories on any topic they like. The submission of the stories should be done online.

Rules

  • Entries must be in English only.

  • The story must be an original work of the participant.

  • Each entry, at most must be 2500-3000 words in length.

  • Plagiarism is strictly discouraged and may lead to disqualification of the participant.

  • It is an online open event.

  • The stories can be written individually or in groups of two members.

  • The submission should be done online.
