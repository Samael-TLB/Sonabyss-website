About The Dance Club

Dance Club provides an opportunity to break the barriers and display the flair and elegance in one’s frame. It offers a creative outlet to express one’s personality in a creative environment. What’s a better way to open up new possibilities, develop a healthy habit and enjoy oneself other than shaking a leg!

We at Sonabyss'19 will provide you an opportune moment to step up on the stage and let your creativity soar high , with events such as Solo Striker & Street Dance.

Solo Striker

It is a stage where you can speak your heart out. So get up and dance to express your heart. Dance like you have never danced before, grab the opportunity and let your dance speak.

Street Dance

Display your individual brilliance, be the star of the stage that’s all yours. The stage gives you the chance to flaunt your best moves and elegance. This is your chance to free yourself and express the best.
