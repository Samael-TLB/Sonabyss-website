About Photography Club

Be it the Burning Monk of Vietnam or the VJ-Day pic, photography has always been a powerful medium of depicting much more than what words could speak. Ability to steal the tint of time, friendship with light and colors and an eye for beauty and reality and handy post production skills are all one needs. This is us, the Photography Club!

Pradarshani

Pradarshini is an art exhibition, where your skills will be out in the public and the public will get to rate your photography skills. The photographs will be segregated into various genres namely portrait, monochrome, landscape, night photography etc.

Spot Photgraphy

In this competition the participants will be taken to a spot location, and they will have to use their witty shutterbug skills and capture the best photograph using the props available on the location.
