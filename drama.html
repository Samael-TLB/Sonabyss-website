About Drama Club

The Drama Club is a very interesting club whose objective is to provide those with an initial interest in acting and an opportunity to learn more about the art. It is designed for students interested in learning more about the aspects of theatre. There shall be a variety of events focusing on acting skills. And is open to all the students.
