Triwizard Tournament

Description

Round 1

  • It is a General Round

  • Each team will be asked 2 questions of 10 marks each. They will be given 30 seconds for each question.

  • If the allotted team is unable to answer the question then the question will passed on to the subsequent teams.

  • Subsequent teams will be given 15 seconds to answer & will be awarded 5 marks for each correct answer.

  • Pounce rule will be applicable.

Round 2

  • It is the Buzzer Round

  • There will be 8 questions in this round. Team pressing the buzzer first will be given the chance to answer.

  • 15 marks will be awarded for each correct answer & 10 marks will be deducted for each incorrect answer.

  • Team will be given 60 seconds for each question.

Round 3

  • It is a round of  Specialized Genre

  • Every team will have to choose their favorite genre out of Movie, Anime, TV Series, KDrama, KPop or Music.

  • Every team will be asked 2 questions from their chosen subjects.

  • They will be given 60 seconds for each question.

  • 15 marks will be awarded for every correct answer.

Round 4

  • It is the Character Identification Round

  • Every team will be asked to identify a personality.

  • Three hints will be provided for every question.

  • 20 marks will be awarded for the correct answer after 1st.

  • 15 marks will be awarded for the correct answer after 2nd.

  • 10 marks will be awarded for the correct answer after 3rd.

  • 60 seconds will be given for each question.

Round 5

  • It is a Rapid Fire Round

  • Every team will be asked to nominate one member from their team.

  • Nominated member will be asked to answer ten questions in 60 seconds.

  • 5 marks will be awarded for each correct answer.

Triple Speak

Description

Triple speak is an extempore speech competition, wherein participants will be given three random words based uponWhich they will have to prepare their speech.

Rules

  • It is an individual event.

  • The participant will have to perform on spot.

  • A total of 5 minutes will be allotted to each participant.(ie. 2 minutes to formulate their ideas n 3 minutes to deliver their speech).

  • Participant will be judged upon their presence of mind, clarity of speech and body-language.

  • Each participant will be scored out of 10.

Alfaz

Description

“Alfaz” is a poetry competition, which provides a platform to showcase one’s creativity and opinions through the art of poetry. The participants are required to read there poems without using props or any background aid.

Rules

  • It is an individual event.

  • The poems should be the original work of the participant.

  • The subject of the poems has to be regarding the socio-political agendas.

  • No props, costume, music or any aid of such kind are allowed.

  • 3 minutes will be given to each participant for their performance.

  • The participants will be scored out of 10, and the decision of the judges shall be considered as final.

Tri Corner Debate

Description

This event introduces a new form of debating. By the end of the event, participants will be able to express their positions, as well as opposing arguments, on a particular issue.

Rules

  • This is a group event, and each group consists of three members

  • Only three groups will be allowed to participate in the event.

  • The selection of the three groups will be based on order of registration. Three groups will be randomly divided into three point of views viz. Agree, Somewhat Agree, Disagree.

  • The topic for the debate will be provided on spot and small amount of time will be provided to discuss the topic among teammates to frame your opinion. 5. Each group must appoint one spokesperson .

  • The spokesperson will be putting forward the group’s point of view.

  • While the spokesperson is speaking, the other two members must formulate their point of view in form of a short essay.

  • In the meantime, other groups must listen to opponent’s spokesperson to counter his/her points in rebuttal round.

  • The judgement will be provided on the basis of speaking skills of the spokesperson, writing skills reflected from the essay, listening skills on the basis of questions asked in the rebuttal round.

Each student will write a 5-paragraph persuasive essay. In order to receive a maximum score, the student must express his position clearly, use appropriate logic, and address opposing viewpoints.
