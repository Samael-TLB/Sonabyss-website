About Videography Club

The videography club basically focus on making videos and short films. The club gives you a chance to present your innovate ideas and showcase your talent. The club organises the video making contests which involves making of short films, music videos, advertisements, trailer spoofs, and tiktok. Video allows us to tell the great stories about ourself and our surrounding. It allows the people around the world to exchange their ideas and bring out the desired effect on the viewer. So, capture the moment, show your hidden skills and grab the golden opportunity.
