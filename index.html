About Sonabyss

Sonabyss, now in its 32nd edition, is one of oldest college fest of Northeast India and the largest in Arunachal Pradesh. The annular cultural fest of NERIST had seen the likes of Trance Me Campus with Hiz7, Lord of Rocks, Coke Studio with Parvaaz, The Gluttons, Underground Authority, etc., gracing the stage with striking performances in the last few years.
The theme for this edition is- 'Autumnal Symphony'. Just as after surviving the wrath of scorching summer sun, autumn comes with relief and provides to rejuvenate our souls with rest and revivification, the current bonanza of extravagant display of music and culture delves the same objective. Sonabyss has emerged as a platform that provides countless opportunities to the youth for exhibition of talents and skills.
